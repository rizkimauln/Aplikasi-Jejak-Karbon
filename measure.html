Living Sustainably

Healthy Living

  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat healthy food
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Sleep well
  • Avoid stress
  • Limit alcohol intake

Sustainable Living

  • Recycle
  • Drive less
  • Go paperless
  • Eat less meat
  • Grow your own produce
  • Save water
  • Consume fewer products overall
  • Purchase eco-friendly goods
  • Create less food waste
  • Reach out to your local government officials

Reducing Carbon Footprint

  • Insulate your home
  • Switch to renewables
  • Buy energy efficient appliances
  • Use less water
  • Change your diet
  • Turn off the lights
  • Go digital
  • Cycle to work
  • Turn down your water heater to 120˚F
  • Lower your thermostat in winter and raise it in summer
  • Turn off lights and unplug appliances when not in use
  • Change incandescent light bulbs
  • Use a low-flow showerhead
  • Look for an ENERGY STAR® symbol when buying new products
